## Introduction

After commissioning and optimizing **500+ sites**, here are patterns that show up again and again in the field — not always in textbooks.

## Site modernization

Upgrades like **4T4R**, **8T8R**, and **UBBP expansions** aren't just hardware swaps. KPI validation and alarm clearance after software upgrades are where projects succeed or slip.

## Parameter optimization

Power, tilt, RET, and beam configurations directly affect throughput. Small changes at scale create large network-wide effects.

## Working across teams

Transmission, core, and OSS coordination matters during incidents — especially nationwide fiber cuts.
